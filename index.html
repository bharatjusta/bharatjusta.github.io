About Me

I like to introduce myself as a Design Generalist. I am a Game Designer by profession but I am equally passionate about designing User Interfaces and Experiences, 3D Objects(preferably vehicles) and large environments. If I could I would design everything there is in this world. Designing things has always intrigued me. I always view the world as a "work in progress" that has always needed some visual and functional upgrades. There isn't a particular job or field of study that allows me to do that so I decided to be a 3D Generalist. Being a 3D Generalist allowed me to create things in virtual 3D space and give them color, texture and movement. I then discovered Unity and I was already a passionate gamer long before that, so Unity became a medium that would fill life in my creations. A few years and a few job changes later, I landed the job of a Game Designer.
