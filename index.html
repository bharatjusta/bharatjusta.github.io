About Me

I like to introduce myself as a Designer. I am a Web Designer by profession but I am equally passionate about designing anything regardless of being in the boundaries of any label. Designing things has always intrigued me. I always view the world as a "work in progress" that has always needed some visual and functional upgrades. There isn't a particular job or field of study that allows me to do that so I decided to be a 3D Modeler. Modeling things in virtual 3D Environments gives me a bit of peace of mind and I started my journey as a 3D Artist. However, that's not where I want to stop since I know that I can stretch my capabilities further. So I decided to be a Web Designer next. And my design journey continues...
